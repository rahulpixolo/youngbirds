Team

Megha Shrivastava Khare- Founder

Hello Visitor,

I am the founder and owner of this website, Megha Khare. I welcome you all to a unique world that I created for kids – Gilly's World.
I live in Mumbai with my husband and my three year old son. Since my childhood, I had this unexplored inclination towards art, craft, music and literature. In school and college, I was always involved in activities like drama, dance, music, painting, craft, reading, speeches and have won many prizes and awards.
I have varied educational and work experience. I worked as a Kindergarden Teacher and have gained experience in teaching area. In 2007, I opened my own kids activity center in Powaii, which was much appreciate by working as well as at-home parents. Kids Activity classes was an interesting way of learning for kids in a home like environment. I use to teach kids various activities based on Phonics, Sight words, Observation & Recognition, Vocabulary, Reading, Writing, Drawing, Speaking, Singing, Dancing, Matching, Sorting, and Kids & Yoga, in short engaging kids in educational and innovative activities that challenges them to explore their creative side.
I also work as a philanthropist and manage an NGO based in Bhopal – Madhya Pradesh. Primiary focus of our NGO is to provide basic education and support to girl child in chattisgrah area.
I love kids and have deep interest in Graphic and Animation. I always knew, I will end up doing something, which involves children and animation and thats how I came up with idea about YOUNG BIRDS and GILLY'S WORLD.
Being a Mom, I totally understand the importance of early learning and right nurturing. I strongly believe that every kid is born with talent and creativity, as a teacher and parent we need to nurture it with special care, love and most importantly with patience. If we set our kids in the right direction, give them the right tools and support them identifying their liking and passion they will grow up to be happy, contented and successful in whatever they choose to do in their life.
Thank you for visiting Young Birds and Gilly's World site. We hope you enjoy your time here and will visit us often. We are open to ideas and your feedback on how was your experience. So if you have any suggestions then please Contact Us.
Thanks Again and Happy Learning!
